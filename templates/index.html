Knowledge Graph Builder

Welcome to Knowledge Graph Builder

This system converts documents and web pages into an interactive knowledge graph using Neo4j and OpenAI GPT. Extract entities, relationships, and answer natural language questions about your content.

How it works:
  1. Upload Documents: Add TXT files or URLs (up to 100MB total)
  2. Knowledge Extraction: AI extracts entities and relationships
  3. Graph Storage: Data is stored in Neo4j AuraDB
  4. Query & Explore: Ask natural language questions
